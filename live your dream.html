Let Me Tell You The Story About Us

You can also turn your dream into a business just like I did. My passion is crafts. I love to do anything that has to do with crafts. I think I have tried just about every craft there is. One of the things I haven't done is to make my own pottery (but I am thinking about it).

My name is Shirley Atkinson and I would like to tell you how I turned my passion into a business.

My husband was a woodworker when we got married, so he has been working with wood most of his life. I had been doing crafts for a lot of years but had never done woodworking. Well you guessed it! I started doing some very simple wood projects and loved it. I soon advanced in to scroll work and then Intarsia. I was a stay at home mom so I needed a way to supplement our income. I decided to start doing craft shows to make a little extra money. I have many stories about some shows. There were good ones and bad ones and the only way you can find out is to do them yourself. We finally weeded out the bad ones and then we were on our way. They were a lot of fun and we really enjoyed doing them.

The only problem was that they were a lot of work. It took a long time to set up our booth and then to tear it down when the show was over. We weren't getting any younger.
