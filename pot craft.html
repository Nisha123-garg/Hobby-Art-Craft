Pot Crafts



Try designing basic pots with these DIY pot painting ideas to make them stand out! So grab your pots and get started with these inspiring ideas.


If you are using clay pots for these DIY pot painting ideas, be sure to seal them before paintinig to prevent the paint from bubbling.


How To Seal Painted Flower Pots


Clean and dry a terracotta pot and start painting it with waterproof paint using foam brush. Trace a design or pattern of your choice using a stencil and contrasting paint. Follow here.













DIY Nail Polish Marbled Planter


In a big bucket, add water and drops of some nail polish; stir it with a stick and dip the pot in the water so that the polish covers the pot well. Click here.













DIY Terra Cotta Pots


Give a rustic makeover to an usual terracotta pot with gold leafing and paper decoupage technique. Know more about these techniques here.













Pretty Lace Flower Pots


Stick lace patterns onto terracotta pots using glue and foam brush to recreate this pretty lace flower pots. A Beautiful Mess has the tutorial.













DIY Marbled Terra Cotta Pots


Use the marble painting technique to create this marvellous icy terracotta pot pattern in a few steps here. You can freely change the color pattern as your own liking.













DIY Pot Painting


Trace a pattern on a white painted pot with carbon paper and a 3D outliner; later paint the pot with whatever color you wish. Watch thee video to understand the steps in detail.
